How to Remove a GaG

GaG NFTs are soulbound — they can't be transferred or sold. The only way to remove one from your wallet is to pay the 2 PAS burn fee.

Think of it as a "delete tax." The message sticks until you decide it's worth paying to remove.

When someone burns your message, you earn 75% of the burn fee (1.5 PAS) — but only if you chose "Take Credit" when you sent it.

If you send anonymously, you get no future kickback.

Attribution is low-visibility, not true privacy. This is on-chain. Don't confuse "not advertised" with "secret."
